Welcome to Feelings Gift Shop

Founded with a passion for making celebrations memorable, Feelings Gift Shop has grown to become India's trusted wholesaler and manufacturer of premium event decoration items.

Based in Neemuch, Madhya Pradesh, we specialize in providing high-quality decoration supplies for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and all special occasions. Our extensive catalog features everything from elegant balloons and banners to stunning centerpieces and themed decorations.

We take pride in offering competitive wholesale prices without compromising on quality. Whether you're an event planner, retailer, or decorator, we're here to help you create magical moments for your clients.
