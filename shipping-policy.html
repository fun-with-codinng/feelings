Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery across India

Last Updated: January 2026

Shipping Coverage

We ship to all major cities and towns across India. Our shipping partners include leading courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Custom orders may require additional processing time, which will be communicated at the time of order.

Note: During festival seasons and peak periods, processing time may be extended. We recommend placing orders well in advance for time-sensitive events.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

  • Order weight and dimensions
  • Delivery location
  • Shipping method selected

Exact shipping charges will be communicated when you place your order via WhatsApp. For bulk/wholesale orders, we offer competitive shipping rates and may provide free shipping above certain order values.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or SMS. You can use this number to track your shipment on the courier partner's website.

Delivery Instructions

  • Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address to receive the package.
  • Verify the package condition before signing for delivery.
  • For large orders, we may require prior confirmation of delivery timing.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect or incomplete address information.

Failed Delivery

If delivery fails due to incorrect address, unavailability of recipient, or refusal to accept the package:

  • The courier will attempt delivery again within 24-48 hours.
  • After multiple failed attempts, the package will be returned to us.
  • Re-shipping charges may apply for returned packages.

Damaged Shipments

If you receive a damaged package, please do not accept delivery or note the damage when signing. Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. Refer to our Return & Exchange Policy for more details.
